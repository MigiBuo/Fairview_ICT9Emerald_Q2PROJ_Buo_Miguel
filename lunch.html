    • Pork Hamonado Ingredients Instructions

    2 lbs Pork Belly 2 cups of pineapple juice Pineapple chunks Garlic Soy sauce Whole peppercorn Brown sugar Cooking oil Salt

    First, cooking oil needs to be hot. Pour this all over your cooking pot, and then apply heat. Once the oil is hot, you can place your pork belly inside. Fry this for 2 minutes, and then turn the meat over. Cook the other side for the same amount of time. Letting the stew boil, utilize our soy sauce, pineapple juice, half of the pineapple chunks, and water. Put these ingredients in the pot. Then add your garlic, as well as the whole peppercorn to make our dish flavorful. Place the cover on top of the pot, and just wait for the mixture to start boiling. Tenderizing the pork belly, once the liquid boils, you can reduce the heat to a simmer. Cook the mixture for about 60 minutes, or until the pork gets nice and tender. As you are cooking, you may pour more water inside if needed so the stew does not dry up. Seasoning the stew, We may now add the pineapple chunks we have left behind. Also sprinkle in your brown sugar. Then integrate the salt (only if needed). For this seasoning, it’s important that we also don’t go overboard so that the dish does not end up too salty. Cooking the Pork Hamonado, All that’s left for us to do is to keep the hamonado cooking for 2 minutes more.
