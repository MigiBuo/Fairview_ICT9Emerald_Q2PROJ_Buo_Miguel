    Welcome to Pino Y U M, this website is to help others, either foreigners or filipinos, make this at home and to have them experience the hardwork that comes to make filipino food.

We also would like to help spread the wonderful taste of filipino food to the rest of the world to have them experience the delicious wonders of filipino food.
